Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Overview

Clipper is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • TikTok Account Info: When you authenticate, we receive your open ID, display name, and avatar via TikTok's Login Kit. Used solely to identify your account for posting.
  • Video/Audio Content: Clipper captures video from your OBS replay buffer when you trigger a clip. Processed locally on your device.
  • Voice Commands: Audio from your microphone is processed locally for wake word detection. Never transmitted to external servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Video clips are processed locally (transcription, cropping, captioning).
  • Clips are uploaded to TikTok only when you trigger a clip command.
  • We do not store, sell, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not collect analytics or tracking information.

4. Data Storage

All data processing happens locally. Clips are stored in a local folder. TikTok tokens are stored locally in environment variables. We do not operate any servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • TikTok: For posting clips. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • OBS Studio: For video capture. Runs locally on your device.

6. Your Rights

Revoke access anytime via TikTok Settings → Security → Manage App Permissions. Delete all local data by removing the Clipper folder.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, visit chara.is.
